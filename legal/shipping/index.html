Delivery & Shipping

Last updated: May 2025

We take pride in delivering your flowers fresh and on time. Here's everything you need to know about our delivery options and policies.

🌹

Standard Delivery

EGP 80

2–4 business days within Cairo. Orders placed before 12 PM ship the same business day.

⚡

Express Delivery

EGP 150

Same-day delivery for orders placed before 2 PM. Available in central Cairo districts.

🏪

Store Pickup

Free

Pick up from our Zamalek store. Ready within 2 hours of order confirmation.

Delivery Areas

We currently deliver to the following areas in Greater Cairo:

  • Zamalek, Garden City, Maadi, Heliopolis, Nasr City
  • New Cairo, Sheikh Zayed, 6th of October
  • Giza, Dokki, Mohandessin, Agouza
  • Other areas available upon request — please contact us first

Special Bouquet Delivery

Orders for Special Bouquets with custom drawings require a minimum lead time of 7 days from the order date. A maximum advance booking of 3 months is accepted. The delivery date you select at checkout will be your confirmed delivery date. We'll confirm your order within 24 hours and keep you updated via WhatsApp or email.

Freshness Guarantee

All our flowers are sourced fresh daily from our trusted growers. We guarantee your bouquet will arrive in perfect condition. If your flowers arrive wilted, damaged, or significantly different from your order, contact us within 24 hours with photos and we will arrange a replacement free of charge.

Delivery Instructions

You may add special delivery instructions at checkout (e.g. "leave with doorman," "call on arrival"). We do our best to honour these but cannot guarantee them in all cases. If the recipient is unavailable, our courier will attempt to contact you and may leave the flowers in a cool, shaded spot if safe to do so.

Packaging

All bouquets are wrapped in our signature petal paper and tied with a satin ribbon. Special Bouquets are packaged in a branded gift box. Custom illustrations are presented in a protective sleeve inside the arrangement. We use eco-friendly packaging wherever possible.

Tracking

Once your order is dispatched, you will receive a WhatsApp message with your delivery window and courier contact number. Live tracking is available for Express Delivery orders.

Contact

For delivery queries, contact us at hello@petaltales.com or WhatsApp +20 123 456 7890.
