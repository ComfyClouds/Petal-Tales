Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025

Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains how Petal Tales collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our website and services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, including:

  • Name, email address, phone number, and delivery address when placing an order
  • Payment information (processed securely; we do not store card details)
  • Messages and drawing descriptions you submit via our contact or product forms
  • Language and browsing preferences stored locally on your device

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected to:

  • Process and fulfil your orders and communicate about them
  • Send order confirmations and delivery updates
  • Respond to customer service requests
  • Improve our website and product offerings
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Cookies & Local Storage

Our website uses browser local storage to remember your cart items, language preference, and session data. No tracking cookies or third-party advertising trackers are used. You may clear local storage at any time through your browser settings.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorised access, loss, or misuse. Payment transactions are processed through encrypted, PCI-compliant payment gateways. We retain your order data for a maximum of 2 years for accounting and legal purposes.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal data at any time. To exercise these rights, contact us at hello@petaltales.com. We will respond within 30 days.

6. Third-Party Services

Our website may use Google Fonts for typography (loaded from Google's servers). Product data may be fetched from Google Sheets. These services have their own privacy policies which we encourage you to review.

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such data, please contact us immediately.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the new policy on this page with a revised date.

9. Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact us at hello@petaltales.com.
